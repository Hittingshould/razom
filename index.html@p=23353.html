RAZOM HEROES


Today, the sovereign nation of Ukraine has to deal with the most horrendous and brutal invasion. Razom is responding to this by providing critical humanitarian relief  depending on the most urgent needs as they evolve. Razom Heroes is delivering life-saving aid to the first responders and front-line medics.

DONATE TO SUPPORT RAZOM

We are focused on delivering tactical medicine items, hospital supplies, and tech enabled emergency response supplies that facilitate the delivery of this aid.

We send aid to Ukraine on a weekly basis. In the first month of the war, we shipped over 218 tons of supplies. 

CARS FOR VICTORY

Razom has been providing support for frontline evacuations and saving lives in Ukraine since June 2022. The Cars for Victory project is part of Razom Heroes, which centers efforts on providing vehicles for medical evacuations on the frontlines. As of now, our team has delivered more than 200 cars through the project and made 75 trips to the hot zones.

 

We have tried different logistical routes, and checked for the most effective – fastest and safest – ways to deliver the aid. Thus, now we follow a repeatable logistics process for procuring and delivering aid from abroad to warehouses in Poland and western Ukraine, and further to the places in most need.

Accomplished together - RAZOM - in 2022

  • Deployed in-house order tracking software and Ukraine-wide delivery system where Razom drivers take aid directly to the hot zones.
  • Delivered over 500 tons of life-saving supplies totaling over $40M.
  • Packed and delivered over 90,000 first aid kits and over 2,000 medical backpacks, additionally, 50,000 tourniquets–the main life-saving tool–were delivered to medics. Razom has purchased nearly 400,000 tourniquets.
  • We have delivered over 2,000 quadcopters for safe passage and evacuation. We sourced and serviced 69 cars for medics to use as ambulances.
  • We have supplied over 14,000 radios and over 2,000 tablets to keep Ukrainians connected.
  • We sent our heroes 350 wood stoves, 250 generators, and over 100 power stations to keep them warm and connected, winter coats provided by L.L.Bean with generous assistance from Senator Angus King, and nutritious, 3,000-calorie ready-to-eat meals which we developed in partnership with Fest!

As always, we will remain flexible and adjust our strategy as the situation develops with updates here on our site and on our social media pages.  We remain incredibly grateful to the generosity of our community around the world and are proud of our ability to work together to respond to the needs quickly and effectively.
