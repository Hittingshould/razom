OUR MISSION

Razom, which means “together” in Ukrainian, is dedicated to upholding the principles of the Revolution of Dignity (Maidan) and actively contributing to the establishment of a secure, prosperous, and democratic Ukraine. We achieve this by creating, inspiring, and collaborating on initiatives that motivate people to think, partner and do.

OUR COMMUNITY

True to our name, we are not able to successfully fulfill our mission without acting together. Our donors, allies and volunteers form a dynamic community that enables us to support Ukraine in the many ways described by our five program areas. We are grateful for each and every one of our supporters: indeed, together, we are Ukraine.
